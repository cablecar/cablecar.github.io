Cable Car Software is a San Francisco based company that designs and develops software products for ourselves and our clients. We work with technologies like Ruby on Rails, Java, MySQL, Linux, OpenSocial, Facebook, Javascript, and more but those are most familiar to us.

We have over 4 years of Ruby on Rails experience (deployment of applications pre Rails 1.0), over 12 years of Java experience (from Java 1.0) that includes over 6 years of product development on the market leading WebLogic Server, and a history of launching products on time, with quality, and good usability.

Have a new project? We'll get you launched and keep you on track from the beginning to the end of your project.

Have a project already underway and need help? We can help you stay on track or get you back on track.

We provide development services, architecture guidance, code reviews, project management, and development team leadership. We've worked on consumer focused web based applications as well as business and enterprise focused applications.

We practice professional software development. We are test driven. We use code coverage tools. We believe in continuous integration. We believe in being as agile as possible without being zealots. We will tell you when we can help you as well as when we can't or shouldn't because there's a more sensible and affordable alternative.

Our Projects

Following is a list of our projects, all created solely by our founder, responsible for all aspects of the project from design, HTML, javascript, database, production deployment/administration, development, product design, all built using Ruby on Rails.

  • LendingKarma - LendingKarma is a person-to-person lending application that helps people that know one another document and track their personal loans with one another. The underlying technology is Ruby On Rails and includes a number of our custom plugins including a CMS, sortable table, tooltip, ajax list editor/modal dialog, and our e-commerce plugin.
  • SociablePet - SociablePet is a social network for pet owners that resides inside of MySpace and Hi5. It is deployed as an opensocial application and is compliant with v0.7 of the opensocial specification.
  • PitchWire - PitchWire is an online network for influencers and publicists.
  • LoanBack - LoanBack was our very first commercial Ruby on Rails application and deployment in November of 2005.
